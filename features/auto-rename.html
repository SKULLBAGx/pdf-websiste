Auto-Rename

Documents are automatically renamed using their metadata

How It Works

  • Metadata Extraction: The bot reads the PDF's internal metadata (title, author, subject)
  • Smart Naming: Files are renamed based on the document title or most relevant metadata
  • Automatic Process: Happens instantly when you send a PDF - no manual work needed
  • Fallback System: If no metadata exists, the original filename is preserved

Perfect For

Keeping your document library organized, especially useful for academic papers, ebooks, and professional documents with proper metadata. Saves time and ensures consistent naming conventions across your collection.
