How It Works

  • Album View: Click "View All" to see thumbnails of every page in your document
  • Quick Navigation: Tap any page thumbnail to jump directly to that page
  • Visual Overview: Get a bird's-eye view of your entire document at a glance
  • Easy Switching: Toggle between single-page and album view anytime

Perfect For

Finding specific sections quickly, getting an overview of document structure, or browsing through presentations and image-heavy PDFs. Great for visual learners who prefer to see the big picture.
